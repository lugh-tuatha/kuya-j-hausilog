About Us

Kuya J' Hausilog is one of the most favorite breakfast houses in Lipa City that offers Tapa, Hotdog, Egg, Longganisa, Fried Chicken, and Bangus with Fried Garlic Rice.

Kuya J' Hausilog provides the best food that most Filipinos are looking for breakfast. It is one of the fast food chains in Lipa City that is delighted to serve you Filipino comfort food. Nowadays, Kuya J' Hausilog is still a favorite Filipino breakfast due to its tasty food, hygienic environment, and Hospitable Atmosphere.
